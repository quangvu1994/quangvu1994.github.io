Exchange

Trade Yours For Theirs

Exchange is a shopping application built on iOS devices using Swift, Firebase, and Social Login API. The App has a beautiful design providing a super convenenience way for people to search, buy, and sell all kind of items. What unique about this App is the ability to trade items with others unlike other shopping applications where you can't really do that.

Tap Hero

Tap Hero is a fun tapping game that based on a famous RPG mobile game, Tap Titans. In this game, you can tap to kill monster, level up, recruit powerful heroes, and strike down enemies with devastating skills. I developed Tap Hero on js.io multi-platform using JavaScript, and DevKit-Scene game engine. You can download the game here on iOS devices.
